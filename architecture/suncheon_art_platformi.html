suncheon art platform

Suncheon, South Koreainternational competition

People on project:
  • NIKOLAI LIM
  • DMITRI VODOVATOV
  • MARIA ZAHAROVA

IDEA

The basis of the project laid the image of the city fortress, which existed in ancient times and framed by walls. These walls, having a circular shape on the old plans and drawings, we have projected on the plot, divided into 4 zones characterizing the city of Suncheon: Nature, History, Local crafts and Cuisine.

Thus, the circular gallery will link together the traditions of this area, and its transparent façade will be the symbol of Art-platform openness to world and will actively entice passersby into using the trade zone located along the street. The visitor, moving round the gallery will be able to get a thrilling experience, to feel consistently all four phenomena and at the same time enjoy a variety of domestic complex views.
