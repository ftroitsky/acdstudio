POLYGON FOR EXPERIMENTS

Ruined lighthouse, a giant wasteland and eternity - are the polygon for experiments. Our aim is to combine the strengths impressions from nature and strong impressions from art. We offer the maximum use of place spirit and create a large open-air art installation - a village of free artists and their spectators who have left the noisy city.

The main principle of which will meet the installation - it is fragility as a result of respectful relation to the nature. Temporary art -objects will created by artists from natural and recycle materials.

"Poor Art" with use the most simple and natural materials (air, wax, mirrors,lead, paper, plant, neon tubes,cloth) has strong roots in Italy. The Arte Povera movement borned in Italy in the 1960s called very poetic and elusive kind of art.

Like the Arte Povera exhibitions the art on the Sicily edge can became a reflection of the great modern contradictions and how artists today define the boundaries between the material and ideal.

Another expression of fragility can be a digital art - visual and digital festivals in the dark.

The project involves the short-termstaying huts for exhibition visitors and artists with minimal amenities combined with the site fencing and comfortable hotel with 23 rooms on the site edge.
